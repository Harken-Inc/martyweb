# AI Search is Changing Everything for Local Businesses

Your clients' customers aren't just using Google anymore. They're asking ChatGPT "what's the best plumber near me?" and getting instant recommendations. They're using Perplexity to research dentists in their area. They're asking Google AI Mode for emergency HVAC repair services.

**The question is: Are your clients being recommended, or are they invisible?**

## The New Reality of Local Search

Traditional SEO agencies focus on Google rankings. But AI search engines work completely differently:

- **Different Sources**: AI engines cite Yelp, BBB, local news sites, and review platforms — not just your website
- **Different Signals**: Review volume and sentiment matter more than keyword density  
- **Different Results**: "Near me" actually means near the user, not just adding city names to queries
